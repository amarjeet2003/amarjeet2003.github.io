About Me

Engineering systems that scale & stay up

I'm a backend engineer who enjoys the hard parts — concurrency, throughput, fault tolerance and observability. At iOL I design Golang microservices and event-driven pipelines that handle production traffic at scale.

My day-to-day spans high-throughput APIs, Kafka & Azure Service Bus pipelines, Redis caching, ETL with Airflow, and keeping it all healthy with New Relic and Grafana. I care about systems that are reliable, idempotent and easy to reason about.

I work AI-first — Claude and Cursor are part of my daily driver, and I lean on LLMs and embeddings to design, refactor, debug and ship faster. I've also shipped AI into production, from Gemini-embedding deduplication to OpenAI-powered services.
